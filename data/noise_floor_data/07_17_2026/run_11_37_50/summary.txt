How to read this
  noise uV rms  RMS of the channel's own noise over the demod band, in µV.
                It is sqrt(Var(n)) — a standard deviation (zero-mean), not a
                peak and not a per-√Hz density.
  Var(n) mV^2   the same noise as a variance — it IS the Var(n_i) the
                cross-sensor cell prints for this diode.
  vs quietest   how many times noisier than the best channel.
  consistency   'consistent' = same floor everywhere; 'varies*' = the floor
                changes with frequency (usually a 1/f rise down low), so read
                the number as a typical mid-band value, not an exact constant.
